Inventory Optimization & Risk Mitigation Dashboard - H1 2026

Author: Rahima Akter Diti

Role: Lead Supply Chain Data Analyst

This repository contains a strategic analysis and interactive dashboard for evaluating inventory performance and risk mitigation strategies for H1 2026. It integrates modeled data to provide a comprehensive view of supply chain health across global regions and product categories.

Dashboard & Analysis Highlights
Financial Performance: Visualization of total H1 Revenue ($378,000 million), Contribution Margins (25.54%), and regional inventory investment trends.

Strategic Focus: Deep-dive analysis into stockout risks (60% of rows on watch), overstock capital immobilization ($4,469 million), and fulfillment center bottleneck dwell times.

Interactive Data: Regional heatmaps and risk registers allowing users to explore branch-level performance, on-time delivery (OTD) rates, and watch status for 64 branches.

Tools & Techniques
Analytical Modeling: Data processing and performance benchmarking against established 7-day inventory targets.

Data Visualization: Dashboard design focused on highlighting critical risk exposure and fulfillment center efficiency.

Disclaimer
This analysis is based on modeled data. It does not represent internal Amazon operational data or official company forecasts.
